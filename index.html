About Us

Your Home Away From Home at Camp

SAED Kitchen is the heart of nourishment at the NYSC Orientation Camp, Asaya, Kabba, Kogi State. Conveniently located Behind the Directorate Office, we are dedicated to serving healthy, delicious, and affordable meals to every corps member and camp official throughout the orientation exercise.

We believe that a well-fed corps member is a productive one. That is why we prepare every dish with fresh, quality ingredients — from hearty soups and swallows to quick snacks and refreshing drinks — ensuring you always have the energy to give your best at camp activities.

  • Freshly sourced, quality ingredients every day
  • Pocket-friendly prices for every corps member
  • Strategically located at the heart of camp
  • Serving both corps members and camp officials
